Chai Tea Latte Recipe

Description

Simply delicious either hot or iced! This will save you more money than buying the chai. In India, each family has their own way of making it, so you should experiment to find your favorite blend.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Chai Tea Bag
  • Honey
  • White Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Pour boiling water over tea bag in a mug; let steep 4 to 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bag
  3. Stir Honey and sugar into tea to dissolve.
  4. Stir in milk through tea to serve and drink
